Marie Curie

Marie curie working in her laboratory at the University of Paris in 1925

    Here is a timeline of Madame Curie's life.

  • 1867 - Marie is born in Poland
  • 1891 - Marie moves to Paris to study at the Sorbonne
  • 1895 - Marie marries husband Pierre Curie
  • 1897 - Marie's first daughter, Irene, is born.
  • 1902 - Marie discovers pure Radium for the first time.
  • 1903 - Marie and her husband Pierre win a nobel prize for their work in radioactivity.
  • 1906 - Marie's husband is killed in an accident.
  • 1911 - Marie wins her second nobel prize for continued work in radioactivity.
  • 1914 - Marie uses her radioactive research materials to perform 'x-rays' on wounded soldiers.
  • 1922 - Marie joins the French academy of Medicine
  • 1934 - Marie Curie dies due to years of exposure to radioactive materials.
